üí° Key Takeaways

1. Callback 1 (Executor): Runs immediately when Promise is created
2. Callback 2 (setTimeout): Runs after 5 seconds, resolves the promise
3. Callback 3 (.then): Runs when promise is resolved, receives the value

All three are callback functions because they are passed as arguments and called by someone else!
